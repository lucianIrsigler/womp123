Maze Game

How to Play

Welcome to the Maze Game! Follow these instructions to start playing:

  1. Hold your phone horizontally.
  2. Tilt your phone left or right to move the ball left or right.
  3. Tilt your phone forward or backward to move the ball up or down.
  4. Navigate the ball through the maze to reach the finish line.
  5. Avoid obstacles and find the quickest path to win!
